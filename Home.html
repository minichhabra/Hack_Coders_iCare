What happens during the 99% of time pregnant women are not at the clinic?

Pregnancy is a time when the emotional and physical care of the pregnant woman has been shown to have significant impact on mother and baby outcomes.

How do you monitor baby & mother vitals? How could you identify early signs of complication? How do you truly deliver continuity of care anytime anywhere? 

In close collaboration with women, clinicians and university hospitals, we provide a remote maternity care platform to improve perinatal outcomes through mobile technology and data science. We enable healthcare professionals to gain new insights into the health of the mother and fetus whilst offering expectant women more convenience, choice and better information about their pregnancy.
